Mint Museum Uptown

The NASCAR Hall of Fame contains an interactive entertainment attraction to honor the history and heritage of NASCAR. Having a high-tech venue, designed to inform and entertain race fans and visitors, opened in May 11, 2010. Their venue includes artifacts, hands-on exhibits, Hall of Honor, etc. The NASCAR Hall of Fame is maintained by the City of Charlotte, licensed by NASCAR and controlled by the Charlotte Regional Visitors Authority. Honoring NASCAR icons and creating an enduring tribute to the drivers is the goal of the facility, crew members, and others that have impacted the sport at any point of time in history. When you visit, look forward to exploring the vast amounts of material in the NASCAR Hall of Fame. This is an activity fit for people of all ages.
