The Whitewater Center

The U.S. National Whitewater Center is a facility which opened up to the public in 2006. The center has been designed and has currently operated as an outdoor center offering over 30 different outdoor activities. In addition to these activites, events are held by hosts at the center. Whitewater Center hosts international competitions with a variety of sports alongside numerous festivals and events. This is an activity fit for people of all ages, so long as they have the energy to keep up with the strenuous activities.
